Privacy, Data Collection Statement & Terms of Usage

Information Collected from Site

The information we collect from visitors to the site is minimal. Information collected from visitors of www.sigepvaoalumni.org include: keywords, referring sites, file requests, bad data requests, browser information, access to restricted areas, and search engine requests*. 'Cookies' are not necessary to view this site. Information gathered is used to optimize the site.

*Daily summary reports of website activity are only available to members of the Corporation.

Personal Information

All personal information sent to www.sigepvaoalumni.org via forms or direct e-mail are held in strict confidence and treated as confidential information until notification to the sender is delivered and responded to. Information such as mailing addresses, e-mail addresses, and pledges of monetary support are private. E-mail addresses may be listed in the 'alumni roster' for public viewing. Mailing addresses are supplied to other members of the Corporation upon request. Sigma Phi Epsilon Virginia Omicron Alumni & Volunteer Corporation does not sell personal information to any entity. Personal information supplied at the request of a member, later discovered to have been used in an abusive fashion warrants restriction from personal information of members in the future.

E-mail Addresses

E-mail addresses are made public by some members. Sending unsolicited, or mass e-mails using addresses provided on this site is illegal and punishable by law. 'Official' e-mails from the Corporation are sent from 'Sig Ep VA O Alumni'.

Confidential Pages

Some pages of www.sigepvaoalumni.org are password protected. 

Financial Transactions & Security

No financial transactions are permitted to occur on this site. Pledges of monetary support are supplied to the Treasurer using forms. Never include account information or banking information when communicating using forms, e-mail, on or generated from the site. No pages on this site contain SSL Certificate Encryption. 

Images and Trademarks

Trademarked items on www.sigepvaoalumni.org belong to their respected entity. Images/photos on web pages belong to the Sigma Phi Epsilon Alumni & Volunteer Corporation, Inc. Requests to reuse images for other sources can be done using e-mail AVC@SigEpVAOAlumni.org.

Representation

The Sigma Phi Epsilon Virginia Omicron & Alumni Corporation, Inc. does not represent Lynchburg College or Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity, Inc. (Richmond, VA) directly. The Corporation is affiliated to Lynchburg College through its undergraduate chapter at the College. The Corporation is affiliated to Sigma Phi Epsilon Fraternity, Inc (Richmond, VA) by its chapter at Lynchburg College and lifetime members making up the Corporation.
