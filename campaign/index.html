Alumni contributions are at the heart of everything we do. Our communications, events, and support for the chapter all rely on gifts to the AVC. As alumni, we play a critical role in making sure our red door always stays open. We count on you and all alumni to fulfill the promise of lifelong brotherhood.

Your support allows us to maintain our website, publish our newsletter, organize alumni events such as Homecoming and Spring Alumni Weekend, to purchase much needed supplies for chapter, and to support the chapter (both undergraduate and alumni) throughout their endeavors.

Some of our key fundraising endeavors are listed below:

Balanced Man Scholarship

A hallmark of the fraternity. This Scholarship is awarded to an incoming freshman who embodies the Balanced Man Principles. This scholarship is awarded from the BMS Committee headed by the Balance Man Steward.

Ryan Hale '00 Scholarship

In association with alumni and other generous donors, we are working to preserve Ryan's memory by helping Delta Sigma Pi - Nu Chi Chapter fund the Ryan Hale Scholarship. The scholarship is awarded to an individual who embodies Ryan's spirit and desire to learn at Lynchburg College.

For more information about the Scholarship click here.

1901 Campaign

Subscribing to the 1901 Campaign is a way to donate a small amount of money ($19.01) every month that will be used to support the mission of the AVC and SigEp: building balanced men. These funds will be used to put on alumni events in the future and help the undergraduate chapter with future financial needs.

To join the 1901 Campaign, click the "Subscribe" button below and start participating today. When you want to cancel your subscription to this campaign, simply click on the "Unsubscribe" button below. If you would like to make a one time donation to the AVC's General Fund, please click the "Donate" button to the right.
